Welcome to the Home Video Archive

This site is dedicating to the appreciation of physical media - such as Blu-rays and 4K UHDs. Physical media ownership ensures a tangible connection to the films we love, allowing us to build collections to keep forever.

Featured Pick: The Florida Project

Released by Second Sight in the UK, Sean Baker's "The Florida Project" is a vibrant and bleak exploration of childhood and poverty. The film released October 6, 2017 and received several accolades and critical acclaim.
